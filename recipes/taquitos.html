Cheese Taquitos

Easy taquitos using just a can of chicken chunks and some cheese!

  • 1 can chicken chunks
  • olive oil
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • hot sauce
  • corn tortillas
  1. Preheat oven to 350 F and grease baking dish.
  2. Combine chicken and cheese in bowl.
  3. Warm tortillas in skillet over medium heat until flexible. 10 seconds per side.
  4. Spread 1 tablespoon of chicken mixture and roll tightly into taquito. Place seam-side down.
  5. Bake 20-30 min.
