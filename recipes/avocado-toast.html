Avocado Toast with Egg

Description

Avocado toast. You got avocado, you got toast, you got egg. How much simpler could it be!

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices multigrain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado, pitted, peeled
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice, to taste
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • sea salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a skiller over medium-low heat. Crack eggs side by side until white on the bottom layer and firm enought to flip 2-3 min.
  2. Flip eggs, trying not to crack the yolk and cook until egg reaches desired doneness. 2-5 min.
  3. Toast bread slices meanwhile.
  4. Mash avocado in a bowl, stir in lemon juice, cayenne pepper, and sea salt.
  5. Spread avocado mixture on toast and top with fried egg. Season with salt and pepper.
