Baked Potato Soup

Description

Baked potato soup fit for cold nights and christmas gatherings! Who can say no to bacon, potatoes, and cheese in soup form?

Ingredients

  • 12 slices bacon
  • 2/3 cup butter
  • 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 7 cups milke
  • 4 large baked potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • 1 1/4 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Place bacon in large skillet over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly brown 8-10min. Drain on paper towels; crumble and set aside.
  2. Meanwhile, melt butter in pot over medium heat. Add flour, whisking until well-combined. Slowly pour in milk, whisking until smooth and thickened.
  3. Add potatoes and onions and bring to a boil, stirring frequently. Reduce heat and simmer 10min.
  4. Stir in crumbled bacon, cheese, sour creal, salt and pepper. Continue stirring until cheese is melted.
  5. Serve warm.
