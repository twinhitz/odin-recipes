Quesadilla with Black Bean and Corn

Description

Quesadillas, the perfect Mexican staple of any household!

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons finely chopped onion
  • 1 (15.5 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (10 ounce) can whole kernal corn, drained
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 2 tablespoons butter, or as needed
  • 8 (8inch) flour tortillas
  • 1 1/2 cup shredded Moterey Jack Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Cook and stir onion in hot oil until softened, about 2min.
  2. Stir in beans and corn, then add salsa brown sugar, and red pepper flakes;mix well
  3. Cook until heated through, about 3 minutes. Remove from heat
  4. Melt aboud 1 1/2 teaspoons in large skillet over medium heat. Place a tortilla in skillet and sprinkle with 1/4 Montery Jack cheese, then top with 1/4 of the bean mixture.
  5. Place another tortilla on top; cook until the bottom is golden, then carefully flip and cook until the other side is golden.
  6. Repeat with remaining tortillas and filling.
